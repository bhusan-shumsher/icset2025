General Guidelines

  • The paper must fall within the themes and track areas of ICSET2025.
  • The paper must be written in English, grammatically correct, and free from typographical errors.
  • Original research papers are preferred; however, original reviews and surveys are also acceptable.
  • Concepts, models, and results must not have been submitted or published elsewhere (except expanded versions).
  • Experiments, analyses and statistics should be performed with standard methods and well described.
  • Conclusions must be supported by results and clearly stated.
  • Appropriate references with proper citations are required – IEEE style for IT/Engineering, APA for other disciplines.
  • Length: 5–7 pages (technical papers), 10–12 pages (non-technical papers).
  • Papers will be scanned for plagiarism (tolerance up to 35%).
  • If AI-generated content is used, it must be disclosed in the acknowledgments.

Checklist before Submission

  • Manuscript should follow the ICSET2025 template (IEEE standard) – double column, single-spaced.
  • Both source file (Word/LaTeX) and PDF must be submitted. Max file size: 50MB.
  • Check correctness of author names, order, and corresponding author designation.
  • Include short biographies of all authors (below references section).
  • Submit papers via Microsoft CMT (sign-up required).
  • In case of issues, contact: icicset2025@ncit.edu.np.

Acceptance Procedure

  1. Plagiarism checking (Stage 1): Papers checked against tolerance threshold. Revisions may be requested.
  2. Linguistic examination (Stage 2): Corrections may be required for flow, grammar, or typographical errors.
  3. Major/Minor Review (Stage 3): Papers undergo review depending on quality and standards. Multiple review rounds possible.
  4. Final Decision (Stage 4): Paper accepted or rejected.
